What is edhrec?

edhrec is a service that makes card recommendations for Magic: The Gathering EDH decks.

How do I use it?

Enter the link to your deck into the search bar. Note that edhrec currently only supports TappedOut links.

How does it work?

Recommendations are generated by the edhrec bot, which uses a collaborative filtering algorithm to suggest cards for you to add/cut.
