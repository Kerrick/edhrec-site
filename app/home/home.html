What is edhrec?

edhrec is a service that makes card recommendations for Magic: The Gathering EDH decks.

How does it work?

Recommendations are generated by the edhrec bot, which uses a collaborative filtering algorithm to suggest cards for you to add/replace.

How do I use it?

Enter in your commander or a TappedOut deck link and click "Get Recommendations!".
