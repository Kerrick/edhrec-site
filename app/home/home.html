What is edhrec?

edhrec is a service that makes card recommendations for Magic: The Gathering EDH decks.

How does it work?

The edhrec bot uses a collaborative filtering algorithm to suggest cards for you to add/replace.

How do I use it?

Just enter in your commander or a TappedOut link to your deck and click the "Get Recommendations!" button.
